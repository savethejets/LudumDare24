A Game Created in 48 hours for Ludum Dare theme : Evolution

Controls:
T - Restart from level 1
Y - Restart current level
Arrows Move Around
Up to jump
Down to unlock doors and finish levels
Hold D + Collide to delete a Mobi
Hold Shift + Collide to mate with another Mobi
